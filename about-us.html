About NBAN

What is NBAN?

The National Broadband Alliance for Nigeria (NBAN) is a strategic initiative that aligns with the Federal Government's vision to enhance broadband adoption and drive Nigeria's digital transformation.

Who We Are

NBAN is composed of telecom operators, service providers, infrastructure developers, government agencies, and policy makers. Our diverse membership includes institutions committed to advancing broadband access, reducing the digital divide, and fostering innovation across Nigeria. Together, we work towards creating a connected, digitally empowered society where technology is a driver for economic growth and social progress.

Exclusive Service

What We Do

Broadband Adoption & Usage

Promote broadband adoption and usage through channels aimed at increasing internet accessibility and awareness.

Bridging Digital Infrastructure Gaps

Drive key initiatives to address critical gaps in digital infrastructure, access to reliable and high-speed internet.

Reduce Connectivity Barriers

Collaborate with stakeholders to tackle barriers such as high right-of-way (RoW) costs and low device affordability.

OUR INVENTIVENESS

Key Initiatives

  • Project 774

    A nationwide initiative to ensure that all 774 Local Government Areas (LGAs) in Nigeria have access to reliable broadband infrastructure. This project drives connectivity and digital inclusion in rural and underserved areas

  • Digital Literacy Programme

    NBAN is implementing training and capacity-building initiatives to empower Nigerians with the skills needed to harness the benefits of broadband

  • Affordable Devices Project

    The NBAN promotes the availability of affordable digital devices, especially in underserved areas, to ensure equitable access to the Internet

  • Broadband Innovation Centres

    Establishment of centres that showcase broadband technologies, support local innovation, and drive research and development

  • Community Access Points

    NBAN will establish public access points across communities, providing free or low-cost internet access to enhance connectivity for all Nigerians

  • Local Content Development

    Support the creation and distribution of local content to drive broadband adoption and engagement

  • Policy Advocacy and Research

    Engage in research and advocacy to influence broadband policy and address regulatory challenges

NBAN Desk

The NBAN Desk, housed at the Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC), coordinates national and state-level initiatives, facilitating collaboration with local NBAN nodes.

Our Purpose

Why NBAN Matters

  • Q1. Economic Growth

    Increased broadband adoption leads to job creation, innovation, and investments in digital infrastructure.

  • Q2. Digital Inclusion

    Bridging the digital divide ensures that every Nigerian can participate in the digital economy.

  • Q3. Access to Education & Healthcare

    Broadband enables online education, telehealth services, and improved access to critical information.

  • Q4. Rural Empowerment

    Connecting rural and underserved communities helps drive local development.

Become A Member

How To Join:

State governments, private sector companies, civil society organizations, and educational institutions interested in joining NBAN can apply through our membership portal.

Benefits of Membership:

  • Access to Networking Opportunities

    Connect with key stakeholders, including government bodies, telecom operators, and technology providers

  • Policy Advocacy

    Influence the development of policies and regulations that support broadband growth and digital inclusion

  • Industry Insights

    Stay informed about the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities in the telecom and broadband sectors.

  • Collaborative Projects

    Engage in joint initiatives aimed at overcoming infrastructure barriers and enhancing broadband access across Nigeria

  • Visibility

    Showcase your organization’s commitment to broadband expansion and digital empowerment at national and regional levels
