About Us

Introduction to the NBAN

The National Broadband Alliance for Nigeria (NBAN) is an initiative led by the Federal Ministry of Communications, Innovation, and Digital Economy. It is a collaborative platform designed to accelerate broadband adoption, bridge the digital divide, and drive digital inclusion across Nigeria.

The NBAN brings together key stakeholders, including government agencies, private sector players, civil society organizations, and international partners, to ensure broadband connectivity becomes accessible, affordable, and impactful for all Nigerians

Our Aim

The NBAN's Objectives

Facilitate

Facilitate stakeholder collaboration for inclusive usage of broadband

Establish Projects

Establish affordable device projects to bridge accessibility gaps

Promote And Develop

Promote and develop additional sustainable business models that provide a reasonable return on investment

Ensure Connectivity

Ensure connectivity in key government facilities (Schools, hospitals, MDAs, public libraries, etc)

Promote Digital Literacy

Promote digital literacy Programme that drive broadband adoption

Establish Local NBAN

Establish local NBAN nodes in all states of the federation

Foster Local Content

Foster local content creation to increase broadband consumption

Enhance Broadband Access

Increase the availability and accessibility of broadband services across urban and rural areas in Nigeria.

Foster Collaboration

Facilitate partnerships among stakeholders, including government bodies, telecom operators, and technology providers, to build a robust and sustainable digital ecosystem.

Support Innovation

Encourage the use of digital technologies for economic and social development, focusing on industries like healthcare, education, commerce, and governance.

Overcome Infrastructure Barriers

Tackle challenges like high Right of Way (RoW) charges, multiple regulatory hurdles, and high equipment costs that hinder broadband infrastructure development.

Ensure Return on Investments (ROI)

Drive sustainable investment in broadband infrastructure by ensuring that investments in network rollout and technology deployment deliver measurable returns
